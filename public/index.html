◈ Project Methodology

Publishing on the web means something specific: your words, on your domain, portable and permanent. Creatrweb is a framework for building that kind of site with AI tools — without letting the AI flatten your voice in the process. The agent shows options before deciding, stops before breaking anything, and hands final judgment back to you.
