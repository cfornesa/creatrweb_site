◈ Project Methodology

Publishing on the web means something specific: your words, on your domain, permanent and portable. Creatrweb is a practice framework — a way of working with AI coding tools that keeps your judgment in the loop instead of handing it over. The tools ask before they build, show options before committing, and stop before breaking anything you care about. The goal isn't to use AI less. It's to think more.
