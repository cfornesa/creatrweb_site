IndieWeb Platform

Astro IndieWeb Webmentions

Overview

The IndieWeb Platform is a decentralized social networking initiative that empowers individuals to own their identity and content. Built on the principles of the independent web, it leverages modern technologies like Astro and Microformats to create a seamless, interoperable experience.

Key Features

  • Own Your Data: All posts, photos, and interactions are stored on your personal domain.
  • Interoperability: Uses standard protocols like Webmentions and IndieAuth to connect with the broader web.
  • Human-First Design: Prioritizes readability and accessibility for users, not algorithms.
