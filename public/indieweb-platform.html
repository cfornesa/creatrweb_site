IndieWeb Platform

Astro IndieWeb Webmentions

Overview

An experiment in building social infrastructure you actually own. The IndieWeb Platform puts your identity and content on your own domain — not inside a platform that can change its rules, shut down, or sell your data. It uses open protocols so your site can talk to the rest of the web without depending on any one company.

Key Features

  • Own Your Data: All posts, photos, and interactions are stored on your personal domain.
  • Interoperability: Uses standard protocols like Webmentions and IndieAuth to connect with the broader web.
  • Human-First Design: Prioritizes readability and accessibility for users, not algorithms.
